About me

Payments analyst,
data storyteller

I'm a Payment Operations and Data Analyst based in Nairobi, Kenya, specialising in fintech and iGaming. As a Team Lead at Sportserve, I oversee payment method allocation across multiple markets and present weekly insights directly to C-level executives.

My background bridges finance and data — a BCom in Finance from the Catholic University of Eastern Africa, combined with hands-on expertise in SQL, Python, Power BI, Tableau, Looker Studio, and Grafana. I'm currently deepening my Data Science and AI skills at Moringa School.

I care about making financial systems more efficient, transparent, and accessible — and I enjoy mentoring junior analysts to grow in the same direction.

Outside work, I'm interested in capital markets, having competed in the FINTOK Competition by the Capital Market Authority, and I hold CIFA certification (Intermediate Level 2) through KASNEB.
